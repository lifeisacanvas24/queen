About

Coal India Ltd is mainly engaged in mining and production of Coal and also operates Coal washeries. The major consumers of the company are power and steel sectors. Consumers from other sectors include cement, fertilizers, brick kilns

Key Points

A Maharatna company
Coal India was incorporated in 1975 as Coal Mines Authority Ltd after the nationalization of the coal sector. [1] It is a 'Maharatna' company under the Ministry of Coal, Government of India with headquarters at Kolkata, West Bengal. [2]
