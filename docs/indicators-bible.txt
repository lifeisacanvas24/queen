## **ðŸš« FALSE BREAKOUT DETECTION PROTOCOL**

### **Immediate Red Flags (DO NOT ENTER)**
- [ ] Breakout during first 15 minutes (stop-hunt zone)
- [ ] Volume <1.5x average (no institutional follow-through)
- [ ] RSI >80 (exhaustion) or <50 (weak momentum)
- [ ] Doji/Spinning Top at breakout level (indecision)
- [ ] Price closes back inside pattern on next candle
- [ ] ADX <20 (range-bound market)
- [ ] Against sector/market direction (check XLY, SPY, QQQ)
- [ ] Within 5 days of earnings/rebalancing (manipulation risk)
- [ ] ADR exhaustion reached (>70% of daily range pre-11 AM)
- [ ] No volume spike in after-hours (for BTST)

### **Exit Triggers (If Already In)**
- **Volume dies**: 3-candle volume drop >50% = scale out 50%
- **CPR Flip**: Price breaks opposite CPR boundary = full exit
- **Time decay**: No follow-through within 30 mins (intraday) or 2 days (swing) = exit
- **Wick rejection**: Long wick >40% of candle body = take profit

## **ðŸ“ˆ INSTRUMENT-SPECIFIC COMMANDMENTS**

### **STOCKS**
**Float Rotation Rule:**
- **Low Float** (<50M): Require 2.5x volume, use 0.3% stops (violent moves)
- **High Float** (>500M): Require 3x volume, moves slower but reliable
- **Mid Float** (50M-500M): Standard 2x volume rule

**Earnings Filter:**
- Avoid breakouts 5 days before/after earnings (unless post-earnings gap with guidance raise)
- Post-earnings gap >5% + volume >3x + guidance raised = 82% BTST success

**Dark Pool Rule:**
- Check for block trades >1% of float at breakout level (Finviz/Taq)
- No dark pool activity = likely retail-driven fakeout

### **FOREX**
**Session Timing:**
- **Valid breakouts**: 80% occur during **London-NY overlap** (8 AM - 12 PM EST)
- **Avoid**: Asia session breakouts (low liquidity, high fade rate)

**Central Bank Levels:**
- Watch defense at round numbers (1.2000, 130.00) = option barriers
- Breakout must clear by 20+ pips and hold for 4H close

**Carry Trade Alignment:**
- USD/JPY breakout validity increases when US 10Y yields rising

### **CRYPTOCURRENCY**
**Funding Rate Divergence:**
- **Negative funding** (-0.01% to -0.05%) + breakout = contrarian bullish signal (shorts trapped)
- **Positive funding** (>0.05%) + breakout = exhaustion warning

**On-Chain Confirmation:**
- Net exchange outflows >10K BTC/day = accumulation before breakout
- Whale addresses (1K+ BTC) increasing = institutional validation

**Spot vs Perps:**
- Breakout MUST lead in **spot volume**, not just perpetuals
- Spot volume <perps volume = liquidation cascade, not real breakout

### **INDEX FUTURES (Nifty, ES, RTY)**
**Rebalancing Rule:**
- Avoid trading breakouts **2 days before/after** quarterly rebalancing (March/June/Sept/Dec)

**Put-Call Ratio (PCR):**
- **Bullish breakout**: PCR <0.8 (low put buying)
- **Bearish breakout**: PCR >1.2 (high put buying)
- PCR >1.0 + bullish breakout = contrarian warning

**Rollover Week:**
- Breakouts during expiry week require **PCR confirmation**; else 60% fail rate
